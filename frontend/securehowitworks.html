The 5-Step Secure Flow

Our architecture ensures that your files are encrypted locally before they ever reach our servers.

upload_file
Step 01

Upload or Paste

Start by choosing your files or pasting sensitive text directly into our browser-based editor. Your data remains in your browser's memory and is not transmitted until it is fully encrypted.

lock
Step 02

Set Password & Expiry

Define your security parameters. Set a strong password and choose an expiration trigger: either after a specific time period (1 hour to 30 days) or immediately after the first view.

link
Step 03

Generate Encrypted Link

Our system generates a unique, cryptographically secure URL. This link contains the metadata needed for decryption but never the actual password, ensuring true zero-knowledge storage.

send
Step 04

Share Anywhere

Copy the link and share it via your preferred channel—Slack, Email, WhatsApp, or SMS. Because the content is already encrypted, the transport layer doesn't need to be secure.

key
Step 05

Instant Decryption

The recipient clicks the link and enters the password. The decryption happens instantly in their browser. Once the expiry conditions are met, the data is wiped from our servers forever.
