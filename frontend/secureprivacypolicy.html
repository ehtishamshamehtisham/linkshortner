Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This document outlines how SecureShare handles, stores, and protects your data while providing a secure content-sharing platform.

Introduction

Welcome to SecureShare. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at privacy@secureshare.io.

This privacy notice applies to all information collected through our Website (such as secureshare.io), and/or any related services, sales, marketing or events (we refer to them collectively in this privacy notice as the "Services").

Data Handling

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

  • Account Information: We store your email address and basic profile info to manage your account.
  • Usage Logs: Metadata related to transfers is kept for security audit purposes for 30 days.
  • Payment Data: Payment processing is handled by third-party providers; we do not store credit card numbers.

Our Data Retention Principle

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service.

Encryption

SecureShare uses state-of-the-art encryption standards to ensure that your files remain private. We employ end-to-end encryption (E2EE) for all shared content.

shield

AES-256 Bit

Military-grade encryption for all files at rest in our cloud storage.

vpn_lock

TLS 1.3

Secure transport layer for all data moving between your device and our servers.

Because we use Zero-Knowledge architecture, SecureShare employees cannot access your file contents even if they wanted to. You hold the master key.
