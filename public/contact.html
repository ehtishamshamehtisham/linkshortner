Need help with custom QR colors, logo QR codes, QR notes, or custom aliases? Our technical support team is here to help you get the most from our advanced URL shortener features.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a logo to the middle of my QR code?

After shortening your URL, click on the QR code generator and upload your logo image. Our system will automatically place it in the center while maintaining scanability.

Can I change the color of my QR codes?

Yes! Use our custom QR color picker to choose any color for your QR codes. This helps with brand consistency and visual appeal.

How do custom aliases work?

Instead of random characters, create memorable short links like yourbrand.com/special-offer. Custom aliases are available on all premium plans.

What are QR notes and how do I add them?

QR notes appear below your QR code and can include instructions, promotions, or contact information. Add them in the QR customization panel.

How quickly will I get support response?

We provide 24/7 technical support with an average response time of 2-3 hours for all inquiries.
