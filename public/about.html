About SmartLink URL Shortener

SmartLink started as a simple free URL shortener with custom aliases and shareable QR codes. Today, it has grown into a lightweight link management & analytics tool that helps you track clicks, devices, countries, and QR scans — without forcing you to create an account.

What SmartLink offers today

  • 🔗 Create short links with optional custom alias for clean sharing.
  • 📱 Generate instant QR codes for offline and print campaigns.
  • 📊 View a simple analytics dashboard for clicks, devices, browsers, and countries.
  • 🎨 Design QR codes with gradient colors and smooth backgrounds.
  • 🪪 Upload your own logo inside the QR code for branding.
  • 🔒 Privacy-friendly — no forced login, no complex setup, just paste and shorten.
